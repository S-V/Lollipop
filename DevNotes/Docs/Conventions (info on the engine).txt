
//-------------------------------------------------------------------

Math:

 - Coordinate system is left-handed (ala DirectX) with positive y as up axis (0;1;0) (positive z axis directed into the screen).
 - All rotation angles are counter-clockwise when looking from the positive end of the rotation axis towards the origin
 - An unrotated view vector points along the positive z-axis (0;0;1)

//-------------------------------------------------------------------

Units:

  Physics units == Graphics units,
  
  my units are in meters,
  
  1 meter = 1 length unit,
  1 kg = 1 mass unit

//-------------------------------------------------------------------

File system:

backslashes are converted to slashes, e.g. a valid path name: "C:/progs/game/".

//-------------------------------------------------------------------
